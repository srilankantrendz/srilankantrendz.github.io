How to start earning without investing any money !

Hi !! If you are reading on this web-site, it means you are looking for ways to earn some crypto money, without going through the difficulties of using any credit/debit cards. You just want to see whether there’s any way to earn some cryptocurrency without any initial spending of money. I know because this was my mind-set when I was looking for such thing. Trust me, I know. And the good news is : There’s a great way of doing that !!!

HOW ?

Ok, I will quickly tell you the Steps to follow. Within the next 5-10 minutes, you will know it and definitely you are going to thank me for sharing this. Here We Go !!! First you have to download the Brave browser. Brave is a a fast, private and secure web browser for PC, Mac and mobile. It’s a lot like Chrome browser. So when you install Brave, you can simply enable ‘Brave Rewards’. Once you enable Brave Rewards, you starts to get BAT (Basic Attention Tokens) to your account, based on your browsing and ad clicking on the browser. You can totally control how many ads per minute and the max value is 5 /min.

The above is a snap-shot of my personal Brave rewards, which I started to do a few days ago. I have just clicked a very few ads here and there while doing my work and Boom, I’m an owner of few Cryptocurrency (BAT) equivalent to some $. Each month we get the BAT amount into our wallet. The great thing is Brave points (BAT) can be added to an Uphold account (https://uphold.com) and then we can convert it to any supported Cryptocurrency as well, other than BAT or even withdraw money as $. You can convert them to Bitcoins, Ethereum, etc…

Soooo, Start doing this and you will get some BAT (crypto) for yourselves without even knowing it. See, so simple !! You can even transfer your account with Seed phrases if needed. But keeping things simple, you can keep your BAT (money) and transfer to Uphold wallet whenever you need. Then you can play around with converting to other coins and vice versa.

So, that’s it for today. Hope you start earning your coins today and maintain an E-wallet instyle, without spending any money other than a little bit of your browser time. Cheers & see you with more cool stuff again !!!!
